# Content Strategy

This theme writes like a Chinese newspaper special issue: factual, column-based, event-oriented, and concise.

## Principles

- Facts over adjectives
- Short newspaper-like headlines over modern deck titles
- Lead paragraph first: what happened, why it matters, what evidence follows
- Use column labels such as `要闻`, `数字简报`, `一线记录`, `编辑按`, `后续行动`
- Avoid generic business jargon unless quoting the user

## Document Types

### One-Pager

Use a front-page / extra-edition structure:

1. Masthead
2. Dateline
3. Main headline
4. Lead paragraph
5. 3-5 clipping boxes for data, evidence, quotes, timeline, or action
6. One seal / sign-off mark

### Long Doc

Use a multi-page special issue structure:

1. Front-page cover
2. Issue index
3. Key takeaways
4. Chapters as feature pages
5. Appendix / sources

### Letter

Use a correspondence clipping structure:

1. Vertical title
2. Reverse-label theme line
3. sender / recipient / date / place
4. 3-5 body paragraphs
5. Evidence boxes for recommendation letters
6. Seal sign-off

## Tone

Use concrete dates, people, places, and numbers. Keep headlines short and memorable. Do not let the retro newspaper voice become fictional or sensational.
